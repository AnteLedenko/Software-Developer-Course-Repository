Top 3 albums

Powerage
Powerage is their fifth studio album.The album contains nine songs. Songs have unuque riffs and great vocals by Bon Scott, and also this is first album where Cliff Williams plays bass.
High way to hell
Released in 1979 this is their sixst studio album. This album contain their most famous song which shares the same title as the album. This is also the last album of lead vocalist and frontman Bon Scott.
Back in black
Back in black is bands seventh studio album released in 1980. Album is featuring Brian Johnson as a lead vocalist for the first time. This would be the bands most succsessful album and one of the best selling albums of all time.
